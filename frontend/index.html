Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you apply.

What is a Bitcoin Equity Loan?

A Bitcoin Equity Loan lets you buy Bitcoin now and pay over time through scheduled payments.

How is this different from other Bitcoin-backed loans?

This product is not borrowing against Bitcoin you already own. It is financing to acquire Bitcoin over time.

Do I need to already own Bitcoin to get a loan?

No. You can apply even if you do not own any Bitcoin today.

What happens if Bitcoin's price drops significantly?

Your repayment schedule stays the same as agreed in your loan plan.

How do repayments work?

Repayments are made only through stablecoin transfers in crypto.

Is Rhino Asset Capital regulated? Is my Bitcoin safe?

Yes. We are a US company and are in the process of obtaining retail licenses in Florida. Application details and safeguards are provided during onboarding.

Who can apply globally?

We serve businesses worldwide. If you are a person, we need to understand your specific case during the application process.

Is this a loan offer?

# System Overview

Rhino Asset Capital is an orchestration system across eight layers of operational truth:

1. Onboarding orchestration
2. Identity and compliance
3. Underwriting and risk
4. Loan origination
5. Bitcoin acquisition and custody
6. Payment servicing and accounting
7. Delinquency and recovery
8. Communication and evidence

## Business sequence for implementation of the service

Onboarding Session -> Verification -> Requirements and Documents -> Compliance Decision -> Party Creation -> Loan Application -> Underwriting -> Loan Origination -> BTC Acquisition and Collateral Lock -> Repayment Servicing -> Balance Tracking -> Delinquency or Payoff -> Closure

## Operating rules

### Rule 1: onboarding gates everything. No onboarding approval means no party and no loan.

### Rule 2: Loan schedule is not reality

- Schedule = expected plan
- Transaction = executed cash movement
- Balance = accounting truth at a point in time
